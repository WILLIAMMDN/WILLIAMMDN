## Operating Dossier

I build systems where software has to meet real constraints: public transport, education, agriculture, security practice and field-ready prototypes. My focus is not to make demos look busy, but to design products that can be understood, tested, documented and improved.

<table>
  <tr>
    <td width="33%"><strong>Security as structure</strong><br>Privacy, validation, permissions and traceability are part of the product architecture.</td>
    <td width="33%"><strong>AI with a use case</strong><br>Computer vision, datasets and automation should improve decisions, not just decorate a pitch.</td>
    <td width="33%"><strong>Local impact</strong><br>Transport, education and agriculture deserve the same engineering care as enterprise software.</td>
  </tr>
</table>
